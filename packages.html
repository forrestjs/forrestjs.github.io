Navigate the Packages

ForrestJS Packages

Over many many years I found myself configuring some basic stuff over and over again. Nowadays I do my best to abstract generic needs into sharable and reusable packages that you find listed here.

Most of them consist in very little amount of code, but they provide hooks so that your application can easily extend and customize their behaviour.

Core

hooks

This is the core of ForrestJS modularity. It allows you to implement an extensible applications made of composable feature pretty much like Wordpress plugins, but in a Node fashion and with traceable and debuggable support.
