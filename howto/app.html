Universal App

Universal App

The goal with react-ssr is to reduce the amount of time that we spend thinking in therms of client and server, and simply think about our app.

  • What is it going to do?
  • Which features do we want to create?
  • How data flows around it?

The general idea is that our app is the client facing layer of what may become a fairly complex system. In this layer we want to take care of a limited set of responsabilities:

  • UI/UX
  • PWA capabilities
  • Client data management
  • Data fetching

We would like to write as much React code as possible, and as little backend-like code as possible.

We basically want to be free to choose whatever backend technology that better suit our specific need. (spoiler alert: Node fits very very well with GraphQL!)
